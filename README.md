Pharmacy Pricing Calculator

A data-driven **pricing simulation tool** for the Pharmacy Department at ExCare Services Limited.  
It allows management to visualize the impact of markup, volume growth, and OPEX scaling  
on profitability in real time.

---

## 🚀 Overview
This calculator is modeled after the **Laboratory Pricing Calculator** to maintain  
consistency across the organization. It integrates directly with Google Sheets,  
allowing real-time data synchronization for quick business decisions.

---

## 🧠 Key Features
- 📊 **Dynamic Price Simulation**
- ⚙️ **OPEX Sensitivity Modeling**
- 💹 **EBITDA & Net Profit Margin Tracking**
- 🔗 **Google Sheets Integration**
- 📈 **Interactive Charts & Insights**
